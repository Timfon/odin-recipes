Fried Chicken

Description:

Fried chicken, also known as Southern fried chicken, is a dish consisting of chicken pieces that have been coated with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried. The breading adds a crisp coating or crust to the exterior of the chicken while retaining juices in the meat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic granules
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 500ml whole milk
  • 1 egg
  • 100g self raising flour
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 8 skiness boneless chicken thighs

Steps:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients
  2. In a shallow dish, beat eggs, 1 cup of water, salt, some flour, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  3. Dip chicken in dry mixture, then in egg mixture a couple times.
  4. In deep fat fryer, heat oil to 375 F. Fry the chicken until chicken is golden brown, for 7-8 minutes each side.
